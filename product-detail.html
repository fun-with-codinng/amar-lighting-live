Maintenance Guidelines

The ALS-B-01 is designed for minimal maintenance, but following these simple guidelines will ensure optimal performance and longevity:

Battery Care
  • Fully charge the battery before first use
  • Recharge after each use, even if partially depleted
  • Store with at least 50% charge if not using for extended periods
  • Avoid complete battery depletion
  • Replace battery after 300-500 charge cycles (typically 2-3 years of regular use)
General Maintenance
  • Clean light head lens with soft, damp cloth when needed
  • Inspect telescopic mast for debris before extending
  • Lubricate mast sections annually with silicone spray
  • Check and tighten any loose fasteners
  • Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
For complete maintenance information and troubleshooting, please refer to the user manual included with your ALS-B-01 or download it from the Downloads tab.
